About SpinAndAnswer

Rules

  • Users earn points in each game.
  • You can create new questions, but they have to be approved.
  • The games will always be in duel mode (with time) and you can choose a random opponent or just from your group of friends.
  • In case of not responding in time the question will be considered as erroneous, then a following questions will be prosecuted.
  • For each question you will have a time to answer approximately forty-five seconds.

How to play

  • First of all, Login if you already have an account, if you don't, hit the Singup button.
  • Choose the friend that you want to play with, or choose a random rival, and then hit the Start Game button.
  • Hit the roulette to get a category.
  • Then a game with a set of questions of the category gotten will start, and one at the time will be shown on screen.
  • Choose the answer that you consider correct.
  • Try to answer the question before the time runs out.
  • Have fun!
